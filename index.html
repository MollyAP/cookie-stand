Founding of the Company

Pat's Salmon Cookies was founded by Pat, a passionate environmentalist and a lover of sweets. Pat always had a keen interest in sustainable practices and wanted to make a positive difference in the world. One day, while enjoying a cup of coffee, Pat came up with the idea of creating a sweet treat that not only satisfied people's sweet tooth but also promoted the health and sustainability of our environment. Despite having limited knowledge about running a business or even creating a website, Pat was determined to turn the idea into a successful business and set out to make his vision a reality. He reached out to friends and family for help, and with their support and guidance, Pat eventually found great success.

Mission Statement

At Pat's Salmon Cookies, our mission is to provide our customers with the best-tasting cookies made from the highest quality, locally-sourced and organic ingredients, while promoting the health and sustainability of both people and the environment. Our unique and delicious Salmon Cookies not only satisfy your sweet tooth but also support the well-being of our aquatic friends. We are committed to ensuring that our manufacturing processes have a minimal environmental impact, and to become a leading advocate for sustainable practices in the food industry. We strive to make every visit to our store a delightful experience, creating a warm and welcoming environment for everyone. We sincerly hope that we inspire others to join us in making a positive difference in the world.

Helping the Salmon

At Pat's Salmon Cookies , we strongly believe in the importance of preserving and protecting the salmon population in our area. We have formed a partnership with a local salmon conservation group to support their efforts to improve salmon habitats, ensure sustainable fishing practices, and educate the public about the importance of salmon conservation. A portion of our profits goes directly to this important cause, and we are committed to doing our part in ensuring that salmon populations thrive for future generations. As a company that cares deeply about the environment, we understand that salmon is not only an important source of food for humans but also the critical role that they play in maintaining the health of our oceans and freshwater habitats.

All Natural ingredients

We don't believe in using artificial sweeteners or sugar in our cookies, which is why we use Stevia, a natural sweetener that is much healthier for you. Stevia is a zero-calorie sweetener that has been used for centuries and has a much lower glycemic index than sugar, making it a perfect substitute for people who are trying to manage their blood sugar levels or trying to reduce their sugar intake. Our cookies are also packed with other healthy substitutes such as whole grains, nuts, and seeds, which are rich in fiber, protein, and healthy fats. These ingredients make our cookies not only a delicious treat but also a healthy snack that can keep you energized and satisfied throughout the day. Thanks to your buddy Pat, you can indulge in a delicious, treat that satisfies your sweet tooth and supports a healthy lifestyle too.
